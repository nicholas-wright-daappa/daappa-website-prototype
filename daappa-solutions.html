Fund accounting
daappa Core
End-to-end back-office operations for private markets
The proven fund accounting and administration platform. Built for fund administrators and in-house managers who need integrated end-to-end functionality across PE, debt, real assets, and hybrid fund structures. Strong in Luxembourg, Channel Islands, and the UK for 20+ years.
  • Partnership, corporate, trust, FoF, mezzanine, and SPV structures
  • Automated general ledger with GAAP and IFRS support
  • Investor servicing, CRM, and capital account statements
  • Valuations with BVCA, EVCA, and what-if modelling
  • IFRS9, FATCA, CRS, BCL, INREV, AIFMD Annex IV regulatory reporting
  • Pipeline, deal, portfolio, and mandate management
  • Analyser self-service report builder

Data and intelligence
daappa Studio+
The intelligence layer above your fund accounting
The web-based data and intelligence platform for private markets. Works above your existing fund accounting -- or above daappa Core -- without requiring you to change your fund admin arrangements. Seven connected modules, all drawing from one governed DataHub.
  • Extractor AI: 98% accuracy on quarterly portco reports and financial documents
  • DataHub: single governed data warehouse for all fund and portfolio data
  • NAV Oversight: automated 0.5bp check and sign-off. From £199/month. Live in 14 days.
  • Analytics: private markets BI dashboards built on your DataHub data
  • Compliance Monitoring: real-time mandate breach and exposure checking
  • Look Through: ownership and exposure visibility across fund hierarchies
  • Investor Portal: branded LP document and data exchange

How Core and Studio+ connect

Core and Studio+ are designed to work independently or together. When both are running, Core is the accounting engine and Studio+ is the intelligence layer above it. Data flows from Core into DataHub automatically -- no manual reconciliation, no duplicate entry.

1
Core records transactions
Partnership accounting, GL, investor servicing, valuations, and reporting in Core
2
DataHub ingests from Core
All Core data flows automatically into Studio+ DataHub as the primary data source
3
Studio+ builds on top
Analytics, Portal, Compliance, and Look Through all draw from the same governed data

One source of truth

Studio+ without Core
Studio+ connects above any fund accounting system. You don't need Core to use Studio+. DataHub Sync ingests from your existing admin or third-party system.
Core without Studio+
Core works fully independently. Many clients run Core for years before adding Studio+ components as their intelligence needs grow.
Core and Studio+ together
The strongest combination. End-to-end from transaction entry to LP report distribution with one consistent data model throughout.
