The problem

Fund accounting is solved. Everything above it isn't.

Most private markets firms have fund accounting covered. The problem sits above it -- and it's still running on spreadsheets, email chains, and manual processes.

Quarterly portco reports arrive as PDFs
Analysts spend days re-keying data that should take minutes
NAV oversight is a spreadsheet
No audit trail, no formal sign-off, increasingly difficult to defend to regulators
LP reporting takes weeks
Data assembled manually from multiple systems every quarter
Portfolio oversight is fragmented
No single source of truth across portcos, funds, and vehicles
The answer

Studio+ sits above your fund accounting and fixes it.

daappa Studio+ is the web-based data and intelligence platform for private markets. It connects to your existing fund accounting -- or to daappa Core -- and handles everything above it.

Extractor AI processes documents automatically. DataHub centralises and governs the data. Analytics, NAV Oversight, Compliance Monitoring, and the Investor Portal all draw from one governed source of truth.
