The GP challenge

What eats your team's time each quarter

Every GP knows the cycle. Portco reports arrive. Analysts spend three days extracting data. Somebody builds a consolidated model. The model breaks. The update is late. LP queries come in. The cycle repeats.

Document processing is the biggest bottleneck
Quarterly portco reports, financial statements, and capital account statements arrive as PDFs. Re-keying data manually takes days and introduces errors that compound downstream.
No single source of truth across the portfolio
Data lives in separate models per portco, in different formats, with different calculation conventions. Building a consolidated portfolio view requires weeks of work each quarter.
LP reporting takes too long and is too manual
Capital account statements, performance updates, and investor letters are assembled manually from multiple sources. Distribution is an email chain. There is no self-service for investors.
NAV oversight is a spreadsheet nobody trusts
For GPs who have outsourced fund accounting, NAV oversight is a regulatory obligation. The spreadsheet approach is increasingly difficult to defend to auditors and regulators.
Compliance monitoring happens retrospectively
Investment mandate checks, concentration limits, and ESG screens are reviewed at month-end rather than in real time. Breaches surface late.
Technology investment is hard to justify at fund scale
Enterprise platforms are priced for large managers. Most GPs with 2-10 funds cannot access institutional-quality technology at a cost that makes commercial sense.

Common questions

GPs — frequently asked

Do we need to change our fund administrator to use daappa?
No. daappa Studio+ connects above your existing fund administrator and processes the data they produce. You don't need to change your fund admin arrangements to start. If you later want to bring accounting in-house, daappa Core is available alongside Studio+.
Where do most GPs start?
The most common starting point is either Extractor AI (for GPs with document processing bottlenecks) or NAV Oversight (for GPs who need to replace their 0.5bp spreadsheet quickly). Both can be live within weeks and have a natural expansion path to the full Studio+ platform.
Does Extractor AI work with our existing portco reporting templates?
Yes. The Extractor AI proof of concept configures extraction templates to your existing schemas and data points. You don't need to ask your portfolio companies to change how they report. The platform adapts to the formats that arrive.
Can our LPs access reporting through a portal?
Yes. The daappa Studio+ Investor Portal is deployed branded to your fund identity. Your LPs access capital account statements, performance reports, and fund documents directly. You control what each investor can see and when.
