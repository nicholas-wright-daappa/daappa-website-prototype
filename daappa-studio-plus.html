The problem

Fund accounting is solved. The layer above it isn't.

Most private markets firms have fund accounting covered -- whether in-house on daappa Core, or outsourced to an administrator. The problem sits above it.

Getting data out of documents. Normalising it. Overseeing outsourced NAVs. Monitoring the portfolio. Reporting to investors. That entire layer is still running on spreadsheets, email chains, and disconnected manual processes.

Studio+ is built for exactly that layer.

Quarterly portco reports arrive as PDFs and analysts spend days re-keying data into models
NAV oversight runs on a spreadsheet and an email chain that no regulator would call auditable
Portfolio monitoring is fragmented across disconnected Excel models with no single source of truth
LP reporting takes weeks because data has to be assembled manually from multiple systems every quarter
Compliance is reactive because there's no automated way to check exposures against mandates in real time

Common questions

Studio+ -- frequently asked

What is daappa Studio+?
daappa Studio+ is a web-based data and intelligence platform for private markets. It includes Extractor AI, DataHub, NAV Oversight, Analytics, Compliance Monitoring, Look Through, Analyser, and an Investor Portal. It is designed to sit above your existing fund accounting system and connect to it -- not replace it.
Does Studio+ replace our fund accounting system?
No. Studio+ connects above your existing fund accounting system. It ingests data from it, governs it in DataHub, and delivers analytics, oversight, and reporting outputs. daappa Core is available separately for firms that need integrated fund accounting, and the two connect directly where clients run both.
What data sources does Studio+ connect to?
Studio+ ingests data via Extractor AI from PDF and CSV documents, via DataHub Sync from external systems and APIs, directly from daappa Core where clients run both, via the Excel Import Tool, and via Open Banking API for bank data. The onboarding process maps your current data flows and configures the appropriate ingestion routes.
Can we start with just one component?
Yes. NAV Oversight and Extractor AI both work as standalone products. NAV Oversight can be live in 14 days with no dependency on other components. Extractor AI runs a five-week proof of concept. Both have a natural expansion path into the full Studio+ platform.
Is Studio+ cloud-hosted?
Yes. Studio+ is a web-based, cloud-hosted platform running on Microsoft Azure infrastructure. ISO 27001 and ISO 9001 certified. Full encryption at rest and in transit. Granular user access controls and complete audit trail across all workflows.
How long does a full Studio+ implementation take?
Full Studio+ implementation is scoped to your specific data volumes, sources, and requirements. A typical mid-sized implementation takes 8 to 16 weeks from scoping to go-live. Extractor AI and NAV Oversight are available as faster entry points -- five weeks and 14 days respectively.
