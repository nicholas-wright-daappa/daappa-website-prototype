daappa/Studio+/Investor Portal
Investor Portal — daappa Studio+

Branded portal.
Investors serve themselves.

The daappa Investor Portal is a secure, branded document and data exchange environment. Fund administrators deploy it per client. Investors access their capital account statements, fund documents, and performance reports directly -- without an email attachment and without a request to the back office.

Book a demoBack to Studio+
What Portal delivers

Investor Portal in detail

  • Capital account statements generated from Core and distributed automatically
  • Performance reports and IRR data drawn directly from DataHub
  • Fund documents, side letters, and notices with version control
  • Secure encrypted document exchange between administrator and investor
  • Granular per-investor access controls for document visibility
  • KYC and AML document collection and approval tracking
  • SWIFT-formatted bank instruction preparation
  • Investor self-service for document retrieval and history

Why it matters

How Investor Portal fits the Studio+ platform

Investor expectations around digital document access have shifted permanently. A quarterly PDF email is no longer a differentiator. Portal gives fund administrators a client-facing technology layer without building bespoke infrastructure per client.
