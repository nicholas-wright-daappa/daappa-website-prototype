Working with us

Onboarding approach

The daappa onboarding programme is a professional, pragmatic and custom-built approach to help you achieve your business objectives efficiently and with confidence. Our methodology is underpinned by four phases — each designed to reduce risk and maximise value realisation.

01
Learn
Understanding your business
Breaking down the inner process of your business to determine project requirements. From initial engagement through the learning phase, we diligently and collaboratively work to understand your current operating model, your data landscape, and your desired future state.
02
Act
Building your roadmap
Creating a bespoke client roadmap to ensure we meet your business outcome objectives. Based on an agile construct, our proprietary Implementation, Delivery & Governance (IDG) blueprint provides structure without rigidity.
03
Explore
Tailoring the platform
Tailoring our implementation plan to match your specific needs. The explore phase is one of the most exciting in any partnership — whether configuring workflows, building custom dashboards, or integrating data sources, this is where daappa starts to feel like yours.
04
Deliver
Going live with confidence
Delivering tangible outputs and providing early visibility of your desired end state. With a sound base to work from and as our partnership further evolves, we are able to collaborate with you to drive continuous improvement and value delivery.
Working with us

Project governance

daappa employs a standardised project governance framework that delivers certainty across three key dimensions: time, cost, and quality. Our approach is transparent, collaborative, and designed to keep you in control at every stage.

📋
Structured delivery
All projects follow a defined delivery schedule with agreed milestones and sign-off gates. You will always know where you are, what is next, and who is responsible.
🤝
Dedicated account management
A named account manager and implementation lead are assigned from day one. They remain your primary point of contact through implementation and into ongoing support.
📊
Regular steering reviews
Regular governance calls keep all stakeholders aligned. Issues are surfaced early, decisions are made quickly, and progress is tracked against your agreed success criteria.
