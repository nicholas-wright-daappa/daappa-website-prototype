Why join daappa

Supporting your success

At daappa, we are passionate about revolutionising the private asset fund industry by providing cutting-edge technology solutions. We are at the forefront of innovation, continually developing and enhancing our products to meet the evolving needs of our clients.

Your work at daappa will have a direct impact on our clients and the private asset fund industry as a whole. We value people who take ownership, ask good questions, and want to understand the domain they are working in — not just the tools they are using.

We are proud of our diverse and inclusive work culture, which fosters collaboration, innovation, and mutual respect. We offer a flexible and supportive environment where your ideas matter and your growth is actively supported.

Graduates & apprenticeships

Build skills, build your future

At daappa, we recognise the immense value that fresh perspectives and new ideas bring to our team. We offer structured graduate and apprenticeship programmes designed to give you deep exposure to private markets technology from day one.

Are you a recent graduate or looking for an apprenticeship? We offer structured intake programmes in software engineering, client operations, and data management. You will work alongside experienced practitioners, take on real responsibility early, and have a clear development plan from the start. We take the long view on our graduates — many of our senior team members joined us at this stage.
